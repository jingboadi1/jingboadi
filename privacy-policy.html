Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

Effective Date: January 1, 2024 | Version: 2.0

Beijing Jingbaodi Agricultural Science & Technology Co., Ltd. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our mobile applications, or engage with our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account on our mobile application
  • Subscribe to our newsletter or marketing communications
  • Fill out contact forms on our website
  • Make purchases or transactions through our platforms
  • Participate in surveys, contests, or promotions
  • Contact our customer support team

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, payment information, and other details relevant to our agricultural services and mobile app functionality.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access our website or mobile applications, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Browser type and version
  • IP address and location data
  • Pages visited and time spent on our platforms
  • Referring/exit URLs and clickstream data
  • Agricultural activity data related to farm management features

2. Mobile Application Specific Information

Our mobile applications ("Jingbaodi Farm Management App") available on Google Play and Apple App Store may collect additional information to provide enhanced agricultural services:

  • GPS location data for farm mapping and field management
  • Camera access for crop disease identification
  • Storage access for saving farm records and documents
  • Notification permissions for weather alerts and service updates
  • Agricultural data including crop types, planting schedules, harvest records

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our agricultural technology services
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send promotional communications about our products and services
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and customer service requests
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Personalize and improve your experience on our platforms
  • Provide targeted advertising for agricultural products and services
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors who assist in operating our website and applications
  • Business Partners: With agricultural suppliers, distributors, and partners to provide integrated services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business Transfers: In connection with merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Consent: With your explicit consent for other purposes

5. Advertising and Third-Party Analytics

We use third-party advertising and analytics services to help fund our free mobile application services. These services may collect information about your activities across different websites and applications.

5.1 Advertising Platforms

Our mobile applications may display advertisements from the following advertising networks and platforms to support our services:

  • Google AdMob - Google's mobile advertising platform
  • Facebook Audience Network - Facebook's mobile ad network
  • Unity Ads - Game and app advertising network
  • AppLovin - Mobile advertising and monetization platform
  • ironSource - Mobile app discovery and advertising platform
  • MoPub (Twitter) - Twitter's mobile ad-serving platform
  • AdColony - Mobile video advertising platform
  • Vungle - In-app video advertising platform
  • Chartboost - Mobile game advertising network
  • Mintegral - Global mobile advertising platform
  • TradPlus - Ad monetization platform
  • StartApp - Mobile advertising platform
  • 蚊子移动 (蚊子) - Chinese mobile advertising network
  • Pangle - ByteDance's global video advertising platform
  • Smaato - Global mobile advertising exchange
  • InMobi - Enterprise marketing and advertising platform
  • Mopub - Mobile ad mediation platform

5.2 Advertisement Types

Our applications may display the following types of advertisements:

  • Splash Ads (Splash Screen Ads): Full-screen advertisements displayed when the application launches
  • Rewarded Video Ads: Video advertisements that users can watch to receive in-app rewards or benefits
  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen advertisements displayed at natural transition points in the application
  • Banner Ads: Rectangular display advertisements shown at the top or bottom of screen
  • Native Ads: Advertisements that match the form and function of the application interface
  • Playable Ads: Interactive advertisements allowing users to preview application features

5.3 Analytics Services

We use analytics services to understand how users interact with our applications:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Adjust
  • AppsFlyer
  • Branch
  • Amplitude
  • Mixpanel

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Secure data centers with physical security
  • Employee training on data protection practices

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Agricultural data may be retained longer to provide historical farming records and analytics.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Restrict Processing: Request limitation of data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@jingboadi.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13 (or applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

For users in the European Economic Area, the minimum age for data consent is 16 years old, unless parental consent is obtained at an earlier age as permitted by local law.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Binding Corporate Rules for intra-group transfers
  • Compliance with applicable data protection frameworks

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected about you
  • Know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale of personal information
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

12. European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to be informed about data collection and processing
  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Rights related to automated decision-making

Our Data Protection Officer can be contacted at support@jingboadi.com.

13. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
