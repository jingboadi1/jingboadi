Our Culture

Cultivating Excellence & Innovation

Our core values drive everything we do, from developing cutting-edge agricultural technologies to providing exceptional service to farmers worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive agricultural solutions that empower farmers worldwide, combining innovation, sustainability, and technology to create a more productive agricultural industry.

Our Vision

To become the global leader in agricultural technology and services, setting new standards for sustainable farming while ensuring food security for future generations.

Our Values

Innovation drives us forward. Sustainability guides our practices. Integrity defines our relationships. Excellence is our standard.

Our Journey

Company Milestones

From our founding to today, every step has been guided by our commitment to agricultural excellence.

2015

Company Founded

Beijing Jingbaodi established with a vision to revolutionize agriculture through technology.

2017

R&D Center Launch

Opened our dedicated research and development center focusing on sustainable farming technologies.

2019

Cultivation Expansion

Expanded operations to include fruit, vegetable, and medicinal herb cultivation covering over 500 hectares.

2021

Mobile App Launch

Released our innovative mobile farm management application on Google Play and App Store.

2023

Global Expansion

Expanded services to serve customers across 15+ countries with multi-language support.
